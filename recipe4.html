Orlean Roasted Wings




  • Ingredients:



  • 30g Beef or Pork slices

  • 2.5ml Oi

  • 5ml Soy Sauce

  • 5ml Dark Soy Sauce (Just for the color. Not a lot of salt in there.)

  • 1g Salt (Optional)


Directions:


Step 1:



Put 2.5ml vegetable oil, 5ml soy sauce, 5ml black soy sauce and 1g salt (optional) in a bowl, mix well with the beef slices and marinate for 10-20 minutes.

Step 2:



Wash the coriander and green pepper and cut into strips.

Step 3:



Put oil in a pan and stir fry the green pepper.

Step 4:



Add meat and continue to stir fry.

Step 5:



When the meat is cooked, add the coriander and continue to cook until the coriander is tender.

Step 6:



Serve with rice or other staple foods.
