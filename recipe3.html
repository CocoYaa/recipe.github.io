Scrambled Eggs with Tomatoes




  • Ingredients:



  • 1 Tomato

  • 2 Eggs

  • 10ml Black Vinegar

  • 20ml Tomato sauce

  • 1ml Salt

  • 15ml Sugar


Directions:


Step 1:



Wash and chop the tomatoes and scallions. Break the eggs into a bowl and beat well.

Step 2:



Put the oil in the wok and fry the eggs until cooked. Set aside.

Step 3:



Put a small amount of oil in a pan and add the scallions and tomatoes. Add 10ml of black vinegar, 20ml of tomato paste, 1ml of salt and 15ml of sugar, stirring well.

Step 4:



Pour in the scrambled eggs and mix well.

Step 5:



Serve with rice or other staple foods.
